Local Product Of Santubong

At the local wet market many fishermen sell their daily fresh fish that they got from the sea. Some sell fish that has been cut while some sell per kilo.

This is local Handcraft store. As you can see what they sell is souvenir and clothing. Not just that they also sell Batik Pua Kumbu which has Sarawak motive.For a tourist this is a great shop to go because there are many souvenir you can buy at low price!

Along the road in Santubong there are local that sells local snack such as banana or potato chips that they made themselves! It has many flavor to choose and it is also delicious to enjoy while watching the sunset on the Damai beach.
